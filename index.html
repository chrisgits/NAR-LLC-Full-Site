Nebraska Accident Reconstruction, LLC

A partnership of five professional crash reconstructionists dedicated to the science of traffic crash reconstruction.

NAR, LLC partners reside in various locations in Nebraska which allows us to easily respond with one or more members and cover a large part of the Nebraska region and surrounding states. The complexity of the case will determine how much manpower is needed to most effectively complete the reconstruction. In most cases we are able to have one or more of our partners respond to the scene as quickly as possible. All five partners have achieved ACTAR Accreditation. Combined, NAR partners have over 60 years of experience in crash and crime scene investigations and analysis.

ACTAR is an organization that encourages the integrity, consistency and professionalism of those involved in traffic crash investigation.
